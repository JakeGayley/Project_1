Power Sources


There are mulitplePower Sources that you can choose from. the least expensive is spring powered guns, but the down side is unless it's not a sniper rifle it sucks. The next stage up is an AEG which stands for Automatic Electric Gun, these are pretty reliable and very upgradable. Next is Green Gas, these can get a bit pricey but are extremely reliable and add realistic functions. Green Gas is sold in presserized cans and is inserted into the mags of the gun that uses it to operate. The most expensive option is HPA, High Pressured Air. HPA is a speedsofters best friend and worst nightmare at the same time because it shoots really fast but is expensive to refill the tanks.

The Gear


The Gear you own depends on your playing styles, if you find yourself playing in outdoor, open worlds, or milsim(military simulation) events then you probably would own Surplus BDU's for every location as well as various military hardware items such as nightvision and plate carriers. If you play in indoor arenas you'll probably just have gym shorts, hoodies, and a plate carrier, but no matter if you play outdoor or indoor, you still want to have a fast helmet for cameras and fashlights as well as protection from objects around you, this video is why I wear one.

Airsoft Guns


Airsoft Guns that you choose is all up to you. If you enjoy close quarter gameplay then I suggest a PDW(Personal Defense Weapon) where as if you prefer long distance mayhem you'd run with a sniper. if you don't like reloading then pick an LMG, but keep in mind that they're ridiculously expensive. If your thing is medium range engagements with the capability of close quarters then I recommend an assault rifle.

Attachments


Attachments are ment to enhance the look of your gun as well as your performance during gameplay, such as a better grip on your gun or illuminating dark areas of the field. grenade launchers are perfect clearing rooms and can be mounted in the bottom of your guns rails. foregrips help you keep a stable grip on you airsoft guns. flashlights and lasers are fantastic for close quarters and dark areas.

Playing Styles


Your Playing Styles will differentiate from field to field. when playing indoor you'll notice that there is a lot of running and non-stop action, this is known to most airsofters as speedsoft. when playing at outdoor events you will see those people with thousands of dollars worth of authentic military gear, those are MILSIMers or "Geardos". and you will also notice the normal airsofters who wear military gear but to an extent. you can choose what you want to be. Geardo, normal, or Speedsofter.
